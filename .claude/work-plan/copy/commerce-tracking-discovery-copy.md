## Brand Voice Notes

- Wine is personal. Use "your" frequently — "your wine," "your taste," "your order."
- Avoid clinical language. "Not available nearby" beats "Out of stock in your area."
- Time beats distance. "Ready in ~30 min" is warmer than "1.2 miles away."
- Celebrate small moments. The order confirmation isn't a receipt — it's the start of anticipation.
- Feedback isn't grading. "How was your wine?" not "Rate this product."
